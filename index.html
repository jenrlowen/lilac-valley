A Home Away From Home

Located in the hills of the Blue Mountains, Lilac Valley Farmstay offers nothing less than relaxation and enjoyment.

Tired of the bustling city? We have your back. Here at Lilac Valley Farmstay, serenity is all you will know. Laze about in our farm style accommodation, or get out and about. There’s lots to do around the farm! Interact with the animals or enjoy some time in the gardens. We guarantee that there’s an activity for everyone.
